\question Consider the following function.
$$
S(x) = 
\begin{cases}
x+1, & -2 \leq x \leq -1 \\
x^3-2x+1, & -1 \leq x \leq 1 \\
x-1, & ~~1 \leq x \leq 2 
\end{cases}
$$
\begin{parts}
\part List the needed conditions for $S(x)$ to be a natural cubic spline. \vfill
\part List all ways in which $S(x)$ fails to be a natural cubic spline. \vfill
\end{parts}
\end{questions}
